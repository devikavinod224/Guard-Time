## Features

### **1. 24/7 Digital Guardian: Proactive Safety Monitoring**

Move beyond basic checks. Our suite offers a proactive, multi-layered safety net that operates around the clock.

*   **Live Screen View:** Mirror your child's active screen in real-time. See what they see, as they see it.
*   **Notification Sync:** Get parallel alerts for messages & activity from WhatsApp, Facebook, Snapchat, and other apps directly to your dashboard.
*   **Remote Environment Check:** Instantly activate the front or rear camera of your child's device for a safety check‚Äîanytime, anywhere.
*   **Ambient Audio Listen-In:** Hear the immediate surroundings through your child's device microphone with one-way audio streaming.
*   **Smart Content Scanner:** Define custom keywords. We'll scan social media and app content for harmful material and trigger instant alerts on detection.

### **2. Intelligent Location Awareness: More Than Just a Dot on a Map**

Transform location data into meaningful insight and automated safety boundaries.

*   **Live Location Pulse:** Watch your child's real-time position update dynamically on an interactive map.
*   **Timeline Travel Log:** Review a complete history of movements, retracing steps by hour, day, or week.
*   **Smart Geofencing:** Draw virtual boundaries (home, school, parks). Receive instant notifications for **"Arrival"** or **"Departure"** events.

### **3. Focus & Balance: Automated Time Management**

Foster healthy digital habits with intelligent, automated controls that manage access.

*   **App-Specific Limits:** Assign daily time allowances for distracting apps (social media, games, video streaming). Access halts when the limit is reached.
*   **Global Screen Time Cap:** Set an overall device usage limit. Apps are automatically disabled when the total daily screen time is consumed.

### **4. Your Command Center: Simplified, All-Around Protection**

Everything you need is organized in one intuitive hub for effortless oversight.

*   **Insightful Activity Digest:** Receive detailed daily & weekly reports on phone usage patterns, top apps, and trends via a clean, visual dashboard.
